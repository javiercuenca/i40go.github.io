Introduction back to ToC

The 'Asset' ontology module represents data about assets. An asset is a physical entity or digital entity that has value to an individual, an organization or a government. This ontology module also provides a representation about the Asset Administration Shell and associated concepts. Includes the administration of the asset concept, the functionalities of the administration shell, assets and sub models that describe an asset.
